Chief Seattle’s Letter 1855

Our connection with the Earth is deep and spiritual. Every corner of this earth and every living being are valuable and deserve our respect and care. Our actions should reflect our sacred duty to protect and love nature. By preserving the beauty and diversity of nature, we ensure that future generations can enjoy the same gifts we have received. The Earth is a gift to us and our descendants, and we must protect it with gratitude and commitment.
